FAQ

The questions dealers actually ask.

Is this compliant? What about STOP opt-outs and quiet hours? +

Yes. Textly is compliance-aware out of the box: sender ID identifies your dealership, every recipient can reply STOP to opt out instantly, and the AI respects quiet hours and consent windows. We handle carrier registration and keep an auditable log of consent and messages.

Does it really use our own number? +

Each rooftop gets its own dedicated business line, and the contact shows your dealership name - not "Unknown Caller." Conversations live with the store, so when a rep leaves, the relationship and history stay with you.

What about Android customers - do they get SMS? +

Yes. iPhone customers get rich iMessage (blue bubbles, typing indicators, group threads). Android and non-iMessage customers seamlessly fall back to SMS from the same number - no one slips through the cracks.

How long does setup take? +

Most single rooftops are live within a few business days. We provision your line, connect your live inventory feed and CRM, tune the AI to your voice and process, and run a supervised pilot before it handles real leads on its own.

Does it replace our BDC? +

No - it makes your BDC superhuman. The AI covers nights, weekends and the instant-response window your team can't, handles repetitive questions, and escalates hot, ready-to-buy leads to your people. Your closers spend their time closing, not chasing.

Who owns the data and the conversations? +

You do. Every contact, conversation and outcome belongs to your dealership and is exportable at any time. The AI is grounded only in your live inventory and approved data, and never invents a vehicle, price, or promise.
